About

T Pranav is a pre-final year undergraduate student with research experience in the field of AI. His present research interests are mostly inclined towards the application of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Robotics in the Medical sector.

He has also worked on Computer Vision-based algorithms for the benefits of the Mute and Blind sectors.
